Ingredients

1 cup whole wheat flour 1 cup oat flour 3 tbs melted coconut oil 1/2 cup almond milk 1 flax egg 1/4 tsp baking pwder 1/4 tsp baking soda 2 tbs coconut sugar 1 tsp almond extract 73% chocolate chips

Directions

Combine all ingredients in blender. Form batter into golf ball sized shapes and place on lined baking sheet. Bake for 30 minutes on 350 degres F. Let cool and enjoy!
